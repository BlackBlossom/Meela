Prepare the Oil
Shake well before use. For enhanced absorption, warm the oil slightly to approximate body temperature (do not overheat). This facilitates deeper penetration into the scalp.
Application
Apply 8–12 ml directly to the scalp. Using your fingertips, massage gently for 10–15 minutes to stimulate microcirculation and improve follicular absorption.
Nourishment Phase
Leave the oil on for a minimum of 1 hour to allow active botanicals to penetrate the scalp and hair shafts, delivering deep nourishment and restoration.
Rinse
Wash off with lukewarm water or a mild, sulfate-free shampoo.

For First-Time Users:
Daily application for the first 14 days is advised, after which frequency can be reduced to 2–3 times weekly for maintenance.
Clinical Outcomes:
Noticeable reduction in dandruff and hair fall may occur within 3 weeks. Visible hair growth may be observed within 12 weeks (3 months).
Frequency:
Apply 2–3 times weekly for optimal results.

Store in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight. Keep the cap tightly closed after every use.
For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. In case of accidental contact, rinse thoroughly with water.
Perform a patch test before first use: apply a small amount on the inner arm, leave for 24 hours, and observe for any irritation or allergic reaction. Discontinue use if sensitivity occurs.
